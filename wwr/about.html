White Water Rafting LLC

At White Water Rafting, our mission is to deliver unforgettable river experiences with the highest safety standards while preserving the natural beauty of our waterways. We combine thrilling adventures with expert guidance to create memories that last a lifetime.

History

Founded in 2005 by river enthusiast Chatt Kato, White Water Rafting LLC began with just two rafts and a passion for sharing the thrill of white water.

What started as weekend trips for friends has grown into a premier rafting company serving thousands of adventurers annually while maintaining our commitment to safety, education, and environmental stewardship.
